FAQ

Common Questions

here are some of the most common questions that we get.

What are some popular tourist attractions in Kolkata ?

Some popular tourist attraction in kolkata include Victoria Memorial,howrah Bridge,Dakshineswar, Kali Temple, Indian Museum, and Marble Place.

What is the best time to visit Kolkata ?

the best time to visit Kolkata is during the winter season, form October to February. When the weather is pleasent and suitable for exploring the city.

What are some famous festivals celebrated in Kolkata ?

Some famous festivals celebrated in Kolkata are Durga Puja, Kali Puja, Diwali, and Christmas.

What is the local cuisine of Kolkata ?

the local cuisine of kolkata is known for it's delicious food like puchka(pani puri), Kathi rolls, and Misti Doi(Sweet Yogurt).Bengali sweets like Rasgulla and Sandesh are also popular.
